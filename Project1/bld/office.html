"By The 1960s, the growing City of Stamford, was in need of a larger building to house its government. The aging Town Hall was too small and was in need of repair. in 1963 the City purchased the old Stamford Electric Light and Gas Company headquarters on Atlantic Street and called it the Municipal Office Building."

"The old Municipal Office Building stood until it succumbed to the wrecking ball in 1996."
