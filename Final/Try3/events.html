Come to Papa 3

This weekend is seeing a large Super Smash Bros. event taking place in Orlando, Florida.

Some of the big players set to attend include Liquid|Hungrybox, Armada|Myran, PG|Zain, eU|Samsora, RCS|Fatality, Wizzrobe, CND|Raito, WBG|MVD, SS|Colbol, 20GX|Gahtzu, 8BitMan, Typo|Sonido, MVG|ScAtt and Spiral|Ryo, though there are of course many more showing up as well.

There are so many, in fact, that Super Smash Bros. Ultimate alone has almost 500 entrants, while Super Smash Bros. Melee is the second biggest with 100 entrants joining up.

This event will have both Singles and Doubles events for the aforementioned Smash games, as well as Super Smash Bros. 64, Project M and Super Smash Bros. Brawl.

It's not all Smash, though, since the event also features Mortal Kombat 11, Dragon Ball FighterZ, Street Fighter 5: Arcade Edition, Tekken 7, BlazBlue: Cross Tag Battle, Soul Calibur 6 and Under Night In-Birth EXE: Late[st].

Streaming is being done at VGBootCamp, Polarity GG, Duo Studios and SSB64.

Northwest Majors 11 results

Northwest Majors 11 is taking place this weekend in Seattle, Washington, and it's got a host of big games and big names set to compete in them.

FOX|NuckleDu, Jimmy J Tran, Justin Wong, Rise|Smug, SB|801 Strider, RB|Anakin, EG|NYChrisG, SB|DoomSnake, Disrupt|Shadow20z, TGS|CeroBlast, PG|Speedkicks, Tempo|Alex Myers, EG|Ricki Ortiz, Princess Ling, EQNX|CuddleCore, BxA|PartyWolf, ChrisCCH, Disrupt|Joonya20z, Rip, CYG|PR Balrog, Trungy, CCG|RunItBlack, Myk, Binchang, Rickstah, RB|Snake Eyez, UYU|Kami, Suiken, Victrix|KawaiiFaceMiles and many more.

This event has pro tour status in the Tekken World Tour status for Tekken 7 and Capcom Pro Tour for Street Fighter 5: Arcade Edition, though those aren't the only games being played.

Other games being played are Mortal Kombat 11, Dragon Ball FighterZ, BlazBlue: Cross Tag Battle, Soul Calibur 6, Guilty Gear Xrd REV 2.

With Tekken 7 at over 160 entrants, Street Fighter 5: Arcade Edition at over 130 entrants while Mortal Kombat 11 and Soul Calibur 6 are each both over 50 entrants, you know there's going to be a lot of hype matches to tune in to this weekend.

Streaming is being done at Team Khaos, Seattle Tekken and Game Works Seattle.
